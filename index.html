About FindIt

Our mission to help students recover lost belongings

Our Purpose

FindIt was created to solve the common problem of lost items in schools. We provide a digital platform where students and staff can report and track lost belongings, significantly increasing recovery rates and reducing frustration.

How It Works

1

Report

Users report lost or found items with detailed descriptions and photos.

2

Search

The school community can search for items matching their lost belongings.

3

Recover

Items are reunited with owners through our organized system.
